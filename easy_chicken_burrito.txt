Ingredients:
 - 1 lb boneless skinless chicken
 - 2 cups minute brown rice
 - 10 soft tortillas
 - 2 cups shredded mexican cheese mix
 - 2 cups water
 - 2 8-ounce can black beans
 - 1 8-ounce jar salsa
 - 1/4 cup evoo
 - 5 tablespoons Carne Asada seasoning
 - 5 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flake
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
 - 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
 
Directions:
 1. mix chicken, evoo, and carne asada in a glass baking dish. coat chicken and bake at 375 for 25 minutes
 2. while chicken is baking, boil the water for preparing the rice adding chili powder, red pepper, black pepper and white pepper before adding rice. reduce heat to low and add rice.  follow instructions on box for preparing minute rice
 3. after rice is prepared, add beans and salsa to rice and continue to heat on low
 4. when chicken is prepared, add chicken, rice and beans, and cheese to tortillas and roll burritos
